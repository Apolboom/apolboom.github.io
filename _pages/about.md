I'm a PhD student at the [University of Gothenburg](https://www.gu.se/en) and [Chalmers University of Technology](https://www.chalmers.se/en/), in the [Logic and Types](https://www.chalmers.se/en/departments/cse/our-research/computing-science/logic-and-types/) unit. My main supervisor is [Christian Sattler](https://www.cse.chalmers.se/~sattler/), and my co-supervisors are [Thierry Coquand](https://www.cse.chalmers.se/~coquand/) and [Chaitanya Leena Subramaniam](https://www.chaitanyals.site/). I'm interested in (higher) category theory and its relation to homotopy theory and variations of homotopy type theory.
